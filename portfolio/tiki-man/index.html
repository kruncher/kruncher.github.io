TikiMan

Chop the world’s tallest Tiki! 4 tropical environments and 16 Tiki Men to unlock!

I was hired to implement this game with Unity and C# from the provided design document. This involved the design, implementation and testing of the software and incorperating the artwork that was provided by the client. The client required integration with various services such as achievements, leaderboards, adverts, and in-app-purchases.
