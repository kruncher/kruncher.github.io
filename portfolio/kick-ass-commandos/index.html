Kick Ass Commandos

Kick ass with your sidekicks in this fast paced action shoot-em-up.

I worked on various systems in this game including input, path finding, mini map, debugging and optimization in addition to providing technical support with Rotorz Tile System along with a pixel perfect camera kit which was used to remove flickering around tiles when the camera moved between screen pixels.

Aside: For more information regarding pixel perfect cameras checkout this post in my blog.
